Programs

01. Skill Training

Weekly sessions with experienced coaches. Shooting, ball handling, footwork, court vision, and basketball IQ — the skills that earn you more minutes and help you make the next team.

02. League Play

Competitive weekend games in established leagues (Thrive, SOL) with certified referees, official scorekeeping, and elite-level competition against top programs across San Diego.

03. Camps & Clinics

Summer and holiday intensive camps. Deep skill work, scrimmages, guest coaches, and team-building activities.

04. For All Levels

No tryout cuts. Whether you're a starter or fighting for a roster spot, SDRavens helps you improve and compete.

05. Offseason Focus

March through October — that's 7 months with no structured basketball. We fill that gap with programming across three seasons.

Our Mission

Most school-team players don't play club basketball — whether it's the cost, the time commitment, or the travel-heavy schedule. So they go 7 months with nothing.

SDRavens fills the gap with quality coaching, competitive games, and a community of players who want to get better. Affordable. Local. Development-focused. For every school-team player in San Diego.
